York/Sheridan Design was a four-year university degree program delivered jointly by York University located in Toronto, Ontario and Sheridan College in Oakville, Ontario, running from 1999â€“2022. This was the first and largest program in Ontario that offered the Bachelor of Design Specialized Honours degree.

Beginning with the class entering in 2019, four-year design students can enroll in a new Bachelor of Design program offered by York University or Sheridan College's Bachelor of Interaction Design or Bachelor of Experiential Design programs.

The joint program has been discontinued.
